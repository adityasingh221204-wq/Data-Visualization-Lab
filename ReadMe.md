# 📊 Data Visualization Laboratory (TE7663)
Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune | Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

An advanced, engineering-centric repository tracking the architectural design, implementation, and deployment of modern data visualization systems. This repository details our end-to-end laboratory progression, covering enterprise business intelligence (Power BI), advanced statistical computing (R Integration), performance-tuned data modeling, and automated data storytelling.

## 🏛️ Course Architecture & Governance

* **Course Code:** TE7663  
* **Academic Year / Semester:** Year III / Semester V (Batch 2024-28)  
* **Credits:** 2 Credits (60 Actual Teaching Hours / 30 Contact Hours Equivalence)  
* **Faculty Panel:** Dr. Sagar Dhanraj Pande, Dr. Shivali Wagle, Prof. Ashwini Tande, Prof. Shruti Sunnad  
* **Department:** Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML)  

### 🎯 Course Outcomes (COs)
- **CO1:** Explain the key techniques and the theory behind data visualization.
- **CO2:** Illustrate use of various visualization tools effectively (like tables, charts, spatial data, time series, etc).
- **CO3:** Design and develop data visualization dashboards for real world data.
- **CO4:** Formulate and tell a story about the real world data through a dashboard.
